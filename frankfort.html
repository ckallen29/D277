Visiting Frankfort, KY

Nestled along the scenic banks of the Kentucky River, Frankfort beckons with a unique blend of history, culture, and Southern hospitality. As the capital city of Kentucky, Frankfort invites you to explore its rich heritage, picturesque landscapes, and vibrant community.

Historic Treasures:

Step back in time as you wander through Frankfort's historic district. Marvel at the grandeur of the Old State Capitol, a masterpiece of Greek Revival architecture. Immerse yourself in the stories of Kentucky's past at the Thomas D. Clark Center for Kentucky History. Stroll along tree-lined streets adorned with antebellum homes, each echoing tales of a bygone era.

Capital City Charms:

Despite its historic roots, Frankfort is a city on the move. Explore the boutique shops, art galleries, and cozy cafes that line the streets of downtown. Unwind in the tranquil surroundings of Juniper Hill Park or enjoy a leisurely cruise along the Kentucky River.

Bourbon Trail Gateway:

For bourbon enthusiasts, Frankfort serves as a gateway to the famous Bourbon Trail. Embark on a journey to renowned distilleries, where master craftsmen create the world's finest bourbons. Savor the flavors of Kentucky's signature spirit and learn about the artistry behind each bottle.

Events and Festivals:

Frankfort comes alive with a calendar full of events and festivals. From the vibrant Capital Expo Festival to the Kentucky Book Fair, there's always something exciting happening. Join locals in celebrating the best of music, art, and culture throughout the year.

Warm Hospitality:

Experience the warmth of Southern hospitality as you meet friendly locals eager to share their love for Frankfort. Whether you're savoring local delicacies, attending a live performance, or simply taking in the charm of the city, you'll feel like a welcome guest in this inviting community.

Plan Your Visit:

Frankfort, KY, offers a delightful escape for history enthusiasts, nature lovers, and anyone seeking a taste of authentic Kentucky living. Come and discover the heart of the Bluegrass State in a city where history meets charm.
